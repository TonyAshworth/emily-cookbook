* 1 onion, sliced
* 1 bell pepper, diced
* 1lb ground beef
* 1 C stewed tomatoes
* ¼ C washed raw rice
* ½ tsp chili powder

Preheat oven to 375. Brown meat and onion, and drain fat.

In a 2qt baking dish, combine all ingredients (including meat and onions), cover and bake for one hour. Remove lid and stir at the 45 minutes mark!
