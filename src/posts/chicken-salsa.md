bs chicken boobs

jar of your fave salsa (get creative here - we love mixing small jars of pineapple salsa and mango salsa!)

can of black beans, drained

can of corn, drained

Toss all into crock pot, and cook for 6 to 9 hrs. Shred chicken and enjoy!
